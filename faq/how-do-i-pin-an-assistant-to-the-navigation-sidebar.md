# How do I pin an assistant to the navigation sidebar?

Click the **Pin** button (pin icon) on an assistant card — available from **Project Assistants**, **Marketplace**, the **Favorites** tab or page, and the **Assistant Details** page. A confirmation toast appears and the assistant is added to the **Pinned** section in the navigation sidebar.

Pinned assistants appear as a row of avatar icons in the sidebar. Click any avatar to start a chat, or click the pinned section block to open a dropdown with all pinned assistants. When the navigation menu is collapsed, a **+N** button opens the same dropdown.

To unpin, click the unpin option in the dropdown, or click the **Pin** button on the assistant card in any of the locations above. Unpinning requires confirmation in a popup.
