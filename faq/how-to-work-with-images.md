# How to work with images? Image processing? How can I work with images? Processing pictures?

CodeMie assistants are capable of handling a variety of image formats, including .jpg, .png, and .svg. They can analyze the content of images, provide recommendations or enhancements, and identify objects within the pictures, among other functionalities. To upload an image into the chat, simply click the paperclip button. Select image. And ask questions. Processing pictures.

The native functionality of CodeMie assistants supports image processing with LLM Models such as GPT-4о Bedrock, Claude 3.5 Sonnet, and Bedrock Claude 3 Sonnet.

These models are inherently equipped to handle tasks related to image analysis and manipulation. However, for extended capabilities with newer or more advanced models such as GPT-4о, and Gemini Pro, an additional tool is required to perform image processing. This tool acts as a bridge, enabling these models to analyze and interpret images, expanding their ability to work with visual data alongside their primary text processing functionalities.
