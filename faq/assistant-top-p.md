# Assistant TOP P?

Top P: Also known as nucleus sampling, Top P is a parameter in natural language processing models that influences the randomness of predictions. It sets a threshold for the cumulative probability distribution of the next word. For example, with a Top P value of 0.9, the model will only consider the smallest group of words whose combined probability exceeds 90%.
