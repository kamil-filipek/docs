# Can I edit or delete my assistant after creating it? Is it possible to customize the functionality of an assistant in CodeMie?

Yes, you can edit or delete your custom assistant anytime. There are multiple ways to edit your assistant:

1. From the Assistants -> Project Assistants menu, where you will find dedicated buttons for editing or deleting your assistant.

2. From the Assistant details popup\*\*:
   - In the assistant list, click on an assistant to open the Assistant details popup
   - Click the "Edit" button in the popup
   - Make your changes and click "Save"

3. From the assistant card\*\*: Use the edit action (pencil icon) available on the assistant's card in various views.

Any changes you make will be immediately reflected across the platform.
