# How to Delete All Conversations ? How to clear my entire chat history with assistants? How can I delete all chats?

The Conversations Management allows you to bulk manage your entire conversation history across all workflows and assistants in your workspace. This feature is particularly useful when you need to clear your chat history for privacy, storage, or organizational purposes.

## Accessing Conversations Management:

1. **Open Profile Settings**: Click on your profile avatar in the bottom-left corner of the screen
2. **Navigate to Settings**: Select "Settings" from the profile menu
3. **Find Conversations Management**: Click on profile icon

## Deleting All Conversations:

1. **Click Delete All Conversations**: Press the red "Delete All Conversations" button
2. **Confirm Deletion**: A confirmation dialog will appear asking "Are you sure you want to delete all conversations?"
3. **Final Confirmation**: Click "Delete" to permanently remove all conversations, or "Cancel" to abort the operation.
