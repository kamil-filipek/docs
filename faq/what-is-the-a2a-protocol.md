# What is the A2A Protocol?

The Agent-to-Agent (A2A) Protocol is an open-standard protocol designed to enable seamless communication and collaboration between AI agents built with different frameworks and by different vendors. A2A provides a common language that breaks down silos and fosters interoperability across the AI ecosystem. With A2A integration, you can connect CodeMie to external AI agents—regardless of their framework or origin—allowing them to communicate effortlessly and work together on complex tasks.
