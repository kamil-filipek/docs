# How do I check my current LLM spending and remaining budget?

Open **Settings → Profile**. The **Your personal spending** section at the top of the page
shows your current spend in USD, the date when your budget resets, and the time remaining
until the reset. A circular progress chart displays how much of your total budget has been
used.

If the widget is not visible, your administrator has not configured a budget limit for your
account. Contact your administrator to enable budget tracking via LiteLLM.
