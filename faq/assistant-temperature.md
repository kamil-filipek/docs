# Assistant Temperature?

Temperature: This parameter regulates the level of randomness in the model’s predictions. A higher temperature leads to more unpredictable outputs, while a lower temperature yields more consistent results. For instance, a temperature of 0.1 makes predictions very focused on the top choice, while a temperature of 2 allows for greater diversity and creativity in responses.
