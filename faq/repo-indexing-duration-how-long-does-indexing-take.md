# Repo Indexing Duration. How long does indexing take? How can I check the progress of indexing? What factors affect the duration of the indexing process?

The current progress and status of the indexing process can be monitored on the Data Sources tab. This tab displays a comprehensive list of your Data Source along with a progress bar that illustrates the ongoing indexing operation. Additionally, any errors encountered during the indexing will be displayed here. Please note that the duration of the indexing process is contingent upon the volume of the data being processed.
