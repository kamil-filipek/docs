# How do I configure integrations when using assistants from the Marketplace?

## Configuring Integrations for Marketplace Assistants

integrations Settings on Marketplace Page

When you access a Marketplace assistant, you'll see:

Information Message

- \***\*Prominent display\*\*** at the top of the page
- \***\*Notification about customizable integrations\*\*** available
- \***\*Guidance on available actions\*\*** you can take

integrations Settings Section  
Located below the information message, this section allows you to:

- \***\*Select existing integrations\*\*** from your personal list
- \***\*Add new integrations\*\*** seamlessly if needed
- \***\*Customize settings\*\*** to adapt to your specific needs

## How It Works

1. \***\*Browse the Marketplace\*\*** and find assistants published by others
2. \***\*Select your own integrations\*\*** from your personalized integrations list
3. \***\*Empower the assistant\*\*** to work flawlessly within your environment
4. \***\*Enjoy global availability\*\*** of curated, ready-to-use assistants

## Benefits

- \***\*Personalized configuration\*\***: Use your own credentials and settings
- \***\*Secure operation\*\***: Your integrations remain private and controlled by you
- \***\*Seamless experience\*\***: Quick setup with your existing integrations configurations
- \***\*Global accessibility\*\***: Every assistant in the Marketplace works with your personalized settings
