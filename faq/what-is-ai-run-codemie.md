# What is AI/Run CodeMie?

AI/Run CodeMie is an innovative platform specifically designed to streamline and enhance the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) through the power of artificial intelligence. This platform is designed to cater to the needs of businesses and development teams, providing them with smart assistance for various SDLC roles and tasks, thereby increasing efficiency and reducing the time-to-market.

This platform is especially beneficial for businesses that are looking to automate and optimize their software development processes. With its extensive library of pre-built AI assistants for various roles like Business Analyst, Team max_concurrencyLead, and Developer, it allows businesses to automate a wide range of functions and tasks, reducing the need for manual intervention.

Moreover, CodeMie provides the flexibility to create personalized AI assistants, enabling businesses to tailor the platform to their specific needs. This can be particularly useful for businesses with unique workflows or those that use specific third-party systems.

AI/Run CodeMie's advanced code assistance outperforms GitHub Copilot Chat, providing businesses with a more efficient and intelligent way to manage their codebase. This can lead to improved code quality and faster development cycles.

In terms of use cases, AI/Run CodeMie can help businesses in various ways - from fast-tracking the onboarding of new team members and analyzing requirements for business analysts, to providing technical task assistance for team leads and autonomous codebase management for developers. It even allows businesses to build their own AI assistant for specific tasks, making it a highly versatile and adaptable tool for any software development environment.
