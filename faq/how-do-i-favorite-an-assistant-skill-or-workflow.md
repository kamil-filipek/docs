# How do I favorite an assistant, skill, or workflow?

Click the **star icon** on any assistant, skill, or workflow card — available from the **Project** and **Marketplace** tabs in each section. The star fills in to confirm the item is saved.

To quickly see all your favorites within a section, click the **Favorites** tab in the left filter panel under **Browse**. If your administrator has enabled the Favorites page, you can also access it from the left navigation sidebar to see all favorite items in one place, with filtering by type (Assistants, Skills, or Workflows).

To remove a favorite, click the filled star icon on the card again — from any location where the card appears.
