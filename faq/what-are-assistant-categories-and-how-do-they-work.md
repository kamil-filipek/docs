# What are assistant categories and how do they work? How do I add categories to my assistant? Can I filter assistants by category in the Marketplace?

Assistant categories are organizational tags that help classify and discover assistants within CodeMie. They provide a systematic way to organize both Project assistants and Marketplace assistants. Maximum to 3 categories per assistant. Flexible classification by functionality.
