# How do I publish my assistant to the Marketplace and what are the credential policies? How to share assistants with external users? How to share an assistant? How to share assistants with integration?

## Publishing Process

1. Create and configure your assistant in the \***\*Project Assistants\*\*** section
2. Click the \***\*"Publish to Marketplace"\*\*** button on your assistant
3. The system will validate your assistant for publishing eligibility

## Credential Policies and Warnings

Personal/Project Credential Detection

- If your assistant uses \***\*personal or project-specific credentials\*\***, a warning modal will appear
- This modal guides you through the process and prevents accidental sharing of sensitive credentials
- You must review and confirm before proceeding with publication

integrations Requirements

- When publishing, your \***\*user integrations will be made available\*\*** alongside the assistant
- If no suitable integrations are selected, you can set \***\*default integrations fields\*\*** instead
- The warning modal will not appear if no user integrations are selected

## After Publishing

- Your assistant \***\*moves from Project Assistants to the Marketplace\*\*** tab
- You maintain full control with options to:
  - View details
  - Edit the assistant
  - Delete the assistant
  - Remove it entirely from the Marketplace
- \***\*Removing from Marketplace\*\*** returns the assistant to your Project Assistants tab
